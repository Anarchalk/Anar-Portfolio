Bio

My name is Anar. I am a recent graduate of the Full-Stack Web Development program at Thinkful. I fell in love with tech industry and it's ever changing nature. Over time I've become completely driven to change the world for the better through innovative new ideas. Although coding can be quite puzzling the feeling of seeing project through to its completion is a marvelous feeling.
